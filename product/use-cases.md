# Use Cases

Primary use cases by stakeholder type.

---

## Use Case 1: Pre-Season Risk Planning

**Actor:** Farm Operator  
**Goal:** Understand risk profile before planting season.

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Register farm boundary and crop type |
| 2 | Execute Maayar assessment |
| 3 | Review composite risk index and domain breakdown |
| 4 | Identify highest-risk domains |
| 5 | Plan mitigation before planting |

**Value:** Proactive risk management over reactive crisis response.

---

## Use Case 2: In-Season Risk Monitoring

**Actor:** Farm Operator / Agronomist  
**Goal:** Track evolving risk as conditions change.

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Re-execute assessment with current conditions |
| 2 | Compare against pre-season baseline |
| 3 | Identify newly elevated risk factors |
| 4 | Adjust field management practices |

---

## Use Case 3: Insurance Portfolio Assessment

**Actor:** Insurance Underwriter  
**Goal:** Evaluate risk across insured farm portfolio.

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Batch assessment across portfolio |
| 2 | Rank farms by composite risk index |
| 3 | Identify risk concentration |
| 4 | Adjust premiums based on risk tiers |

---

## Use Case 4: Agricultural Investment Due Diligence

**Actor:** Investor  
**Goal:** Quantify agricultural asset risk before investment.

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Request assessment for target farm(s) |
| 2 | Review composite index, confidence, decomposition |
| 3 | Compare against regional benchmarks |
| 4 | Include in investment decision framework |

---

## Use Case 5: Regional Food Security Monitoring

**Actor:** Policymaker  
**Goal:** Monitor agricultural risk at regional scale.

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Aggregate assessments across regional farms |
| 2 | Identify risk hotspots by domain |
| 3 | Correlate with production forecasts |
| 4 | Inform policy interventions |
